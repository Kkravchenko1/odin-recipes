Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Salt

  • Oil

Steps

  1. First get the pan really hot
  2. Throw salt and pepper on the steak
  3. Rub the salt and pepper into the steak
  4. When the pan is hot, throw the steak on the pan
  5. wait 5 minutes
  6. Flip the steak
  7. Cook until internal temperature is 130
  8. Let rest and serve
